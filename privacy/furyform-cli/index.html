Privacy

FuryForm CLI privacy policy

The FuryForm CLI generates projects and content templates. It does not transmit or collect user data by default.

How it behaves

  • Runs locally on the developer's machine.
  • Creates files, scaffolding, and optional privacy notes as requested.
  • Does not send analytics, tracking, or form submissions by default.

Use case guidance

If you use the CLI to scaffold a hosted product, include a custom privacy policy for that hosted service or website.
